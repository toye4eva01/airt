Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What is Airtel LMS

A. Airtel LMS is an exclusive club of preferred customers, offering unique benefits to reward top customers on the Glo network. Airtel LMS is our own way of rewarding the loyalty of our valuable customers.

Q. What are the benefits for membership?

A. Members enjoy several rewards and privileges, including

  • Exclusive offers and savings from our Merchant Partners across various segments ranging from Travel, Hotels, Dinning, Shopping, Beauty, Healthcare, Fitness, Automobile, Fashion and more…
  • Free access to VIP lounges at International Airports nationwide
  • Free tow service, free parking and exemption from towing by traffic officials, facilitated by AAA Rescue cover
  • A Free Accident Insurance cover of N20 million.
  • A special round-the-clock information service that provide Airtel LMS members with up-to-date information on Airtel, its products and service offerings, flights, business listings, stock updates, News, events, traffic situation, hotels & restaurants, etc. A short number is given to Airtel LMS members, which they dial in order to access LMS Envoy. It can also be accessed through the Airtel LMS website.
  • Special invitations to LMS Business Networking platforms and high-octane events like Comedy nights, Concerts, etc.
  • A personalized priority customer care on a 24/7 basis through an exclusive hotline for quick and expert help to resolve any issues members may have with their lines.
  • A special LMS corner in major Airtel shops, offering priority & express service by well-trained Airtel LMS staff.
  • Access to International calls and Roaming
  • LMS members are given spouse lines with special rates.
  • LMS comes with a promise…a promise of delightful surprises every now and then…. only from your network of choice - Glo!

Q. How can I become a Airtel LMS member?

A. To join Airtel LMS, you will need to be a Airtel subscriber (this is pretty easy- just purchase a Airtel SIM, or port your current network to Airtel). Thereafter, you will need to spend not less than N25,000(?) averagely on a monthly basis consistently for six months. You will receive notification, inviting you to join Airtel LMS. Please note that Airtel reserves the right to review or amend all terms and conditions for membership.

Q. Can I write or call to apply for membership of Airtel LMS

A. No. Membership to Airtel LMS is strictly by invitation to qualifying customers.

Q. What certificate do I receive to confirm that I am a member?

A. Airtel LMS new members get a special welcome pack, including a special Airtel LMS membership card.

Q. What is the use of my LMS membership card?

A. Your Prive membership card is very important, and you are advised to keep it safe. Presentation of Airtel LMS membership card is required to enjoy Airtel LMS benefits, especially at the various airlines, shops, and other merchant locations to enjoy the privilege treatment that Airtel LMS status deserves. Your membership is like your ATM card in that you need a secret PIN to validate and use it at any merchant location. Please keep your PIN safe and do not share with anyone..

Q. What if I forgot my PIN?

A. You can change your PIN on the mobile app.

Q. How long does LMS membership last?

A. Membership is re-evaluated after every six months, subject to a member’s continual qualification. Membership is renewable, after the six months period, provided a member has remained on the Airtel network, and sustained the spend level that qualified him/her to be a member.

Q. What can I do if my LMS membership card is lost or stolen? Can someone use it on my behalf?

A. Please report loss or stolen card immediately to Airtel LMS helpdesk by calling 1111 or send an email to info@Airtel.com The card will be immediately hot-listed to prevent unauthorized use.

Q. Can I transfer my Airtel LMS card to my spouse or anyone else?

A. No. Membership card is non-transferable.

Q. Are there different categories of membership of Airtel LMS

A. No. There is only one level of Airtel LMS membership for now.
