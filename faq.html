Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What is Glo Privé?

A. Glo Privé is an exclusive club of preferred customers, offering unique benefits to reward top customers on the Glo network. Glo Privé is our own way of rewarding the loyalty of our valuable customers.

Q. What are the benefits for membership?

A. Members enjoy several rewards and privileges, including

  • Exclusive offers and savings from our Merchant Partners across various segments ranging from Travel, Hotels, Dinning, Shopping, Beauty, Healthcare, Fitness, Automobile, Fashion and more…
  • Free access to VIP lounges at International Airports nationwide
  • Free tow service, free parking and exemption from towing by traffic officials, facilitated by AAA Rescue cover
  • A Free Accident Insurance cover of N20 million.
  • A special round-the-clock information service that provide Glo Privé members with up-to-date information on Glo, its products and service offerings, flights, business listings, stock updates, News, events, traffic situation, hotels & restaurants, etc. A short number is given to Glo Privé members, which they dial in order to access Privé Envoy. It can also be accessed through the Glo Privé website.
  • Special invitations to Privé Business Networking platforms and high-octane events like Comedy nights, Concerts, etc.
  • A personalized priority customer care on a 24/7 basis through an exclusive hotline for quick and expert help to resolve any issues members may have with their lines.
  • A special Privé corner in major Gloworld shops, offering priority & express service by well-trained Glo Privé staff.
  • Access to International calls and Roaming
  • Privé members are given spouse lines with special rates.
  • Privé comes with a promise…a promise of delightful surprises every now and then…. only from your network of choice - Glo!

Q. How can I become a Glo Privé member?

A. To join Glo Privé, you will need to be a Glo subscriber (this is pretty easy- just purchase a Glo SIM, or port your current network to Glo). Thereafter, you will need to spend not less than N25,000(?) averagely on a monthly basis consistently for six months. You will receive notification, inviting you to join Glo Privé. Please note that Glo reserves the right to review or amend all terms and conditions for membership.

Q. Can I write or call to apply for membership of Glo Privé?

A. No. Membership to Glo Privé is strictly by invitation to qualifying customers.

Q. What certificate do I receive to confirm that I am a member?

A. Glo Privé new members get a special welcome pack, including a special Glo Privé membership card.

Q. What is the use of my Privé membership card?

A. Your Prive membership card is very important, and you are advised to keep it safe. Presentation of Glo Privé membership card is required to enjoy Glo Privé benefits, especially at the various airlines, shops, and other merchant locations to enjoy the privilege treatment that Glo Privé status deserves. Your membership is like your ATM card in that you need a secret PIN to validate and use it at any merchant location. Please keep your PIN safe and do not share with anyone..

Q. What if I forgot my PIN?

A. You can change your PIN at www.gloprive.com.

Q. How long does Privé membership last?

A. Membership is re-evaluated after every six months, subject to a member’s continual qualification. Membership is renewable, after the six months period, provided a member has remained on the Glo network, and sustained the spend level that qualified him/her to be a member.

Q. What can I do if my Privé membership card is lost or stolen? Can someone use it on my behalf?

A. Please report loss or stolen card immediately to Glo Privé helpdesk by calling 1111 or send an email to info@gloprive.com The card will be immediately hot-listed to prevent unauthorized use.

Q. Can I transfer my Glo Privé card to my spouse or anyone else?

A. No. Membership card is non-transferable.

Q. Are there different categories of membership of Glo Privé?

A. No. There is only one level of Glo Privé membership for now.
